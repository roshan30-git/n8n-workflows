## 🚀 Featured Showcase

### [Steeve Automate: Viral AI Video Agent](./steeve_automate_workflow.json)
Fully autonomous viral video engine. Scrapes AI news via RSS → Scripts with GPT-4o → Generates HeyGen Avatar → Composites split-screen edits via Creatomate.

### [Enterprise B2B Sales Orchestrator](./b2b.json)
End-to-end B2B sales funnel. Voice-to-Lead extraction (OpenAI Whisper) → Dynamic cold email sequencing (Mailgun) → Automated lead tracking (Supabase).

### [Plug & Play Instagram Auto-Poster](./Plug_and_Play_Insta_Auto_Post.json)
Resilient social media engine. Syncs editorial calendars from Sheets with media in Drive → Hosted on Cloudinary → Cross-platform carousel publishing.
